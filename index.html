Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any risk to my business or Reddit accounts?
Zero risk. We operate exclusively through our own network of 100+ aged, high-karma Reddit accounts. Your personal or business accounts are never used, keeping your brand completely protected.
How do you get comments to rank at the top?
We write comments that genuinely add value to the discussion, then use strategic upvoting from our aged account network. This combination earns organic upvotes from real users, pushing comments to the top position where they get maximum visibility.
How does this improve my LLM/AI visibility?
AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are trained on Reddit data. When your brand is consistently mentioned in relevant threads, these models learn to associate your product with solutions in your category—so when prospects ask for recommendations, your brand gets suggested.
What kind of results can I expect?
Results vary by niche, but clients typically see: top comment placement on high-traffic threads (20K-50K+ monthly views), improved Google rankings for Reddit-related searches, and increased brand mentions in AI responses. We provide monthly reports tracking thread performance and visibility metrics.
Is this against Reddit's terms?
We follow Reddit's guidelines by providing genuine, helpful responses that add value to discussions. Our approach focuses on authentic engagement rather than spam. That said, we recommend maintaining a diversified marketing strategy.
Why is there a 2-month minimum commitment?
Reddit marketing compounds over time. A single comment can drive traffic for months or years as the thread continues to rank in Google search results. The 2-month minimum ensures you see the full compounding effect of strategic placement.
